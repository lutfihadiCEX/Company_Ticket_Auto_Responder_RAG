Article 01: Title: How to Reset Your Password Content: If a user cannot login, instruct them to reset their password using the "Forgot Password" link on the main login page. They must follow the instructions sent to their registered email address to complete the process. If the reset email is not received within 5 minutes, advise them to check their spam or junk folder, then try requesting the link again.

Article 02: Title: Updating Billing and Payment Methods Content: Customers can update their credit card or payment method by navigating to Account Settings > Billing. Advise them to ensure the new card details (number, expiry, CVV) are entered correctly before clicking Save Changes. A confirmation email is sent once the update is successful.

Article 03: Title: Resolving "Error 404: Page Not Found" Content: The "Error 404" indicates the requested page or resource doesn't exist or the link is broken. First, have the user clear their browser's cache and cookies, then try accessing the page again. If the issue persists, verify the URL they are trying to reach and escalate the ticket with the specific URL if it's a known resource.

Article 04: Title: Canceling a Subscription Content: To cancel their recurring subscription, the customer needs to go to Account Settings > Subscription Details and click the "Cancel Subscription" button. The subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle. Confirm the cancellation by checking the account status reflects "Pending Cancellation."

Article 05: Title: Troubleshooting App Crashing on Startup Content: For a mobile app crash on launch, instruct the user to first force-close the app and relaunch it. If the crash continues, they should update the app to the latest version from their respective app store. As a final step, advise them to reinstall the app entirely.

Article 06: Title: Locating and Downloading Invoices Content: All past invoices are available for download in PDF format under Account Settings > Billing History. Customers can select the relevant month and click the "Download Invoice" button. If an invoice is missing, confirm the payment date and provide a manual PDF copy via the ticket.

Article 07: Title: How to Change Your Registered Email Address Content: The registered email address can be updated in Account Settings > Profile. The system requires the user to verify the change by clicking a unique link sent to the new email address. This security step must be completed within 24 hours or the change will be reverted.

Article 08: Title: Failed Login Due to Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Code Not Working Content: If a 2FA code is rejected, confirm the user's device clock is set correctly and synced as codes are time-sensitive. If they are using an authentication app, they can try to re-sync it. As a last resort, guide them through the account recovery process to disable and re-enable 2FA.

Article 09: Title: Understanding Usage Limits and Overage Charges Content: Overage charges occur when a customer exceeds their plan's usage limits, which can be viewed in the Dashboard under Usage Metrics. Explain that the current charges reflect usage from the 1st of the month and advise upgrading the plan to avoid future overages.

Article 10: Title: Requesting a Refund for a Recent Purchase Content: Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of purchase and meet the criteria outlined in our Terms of Service (ToS). To process, verify the order ID and the reason for the request, then submit a request to the Finance team for approval. Communicate that processing usually takes 5-10 business days.

Article 11: Title: Adjusting Notification Preferences Content: Users can manage all email and in-app notifications by visiting Settings > Notifications. They can toggle specific alert types (e.g., promotional, billing, account activity) on or off. Remind them to click "Save Preferences" at the bottom of the page.

Article 12: Title: Troubleshooting Slow Loading Times on Web Interface Content: Slow performance often relates to the user's local setup. Advise them to test their internet speed and close unnecessary browser tabs. If the issue persists, have them test using a different browser (e.g., Chrome instead of Firefox) to rule out a browser-specific conflict.

Article 13: Title: Locating the API Key or Access Token Content: The user's unique API Key or Access Token is located under Developer Settings > API Keys. They should treat this key as confidential and regenerate it immediately if they suspect it has been compromised. The token is required for all third-party integrations.

Article 14: Title: My Discount Code Isn't Working Content: First, verify the expiration date and applicability of the discount code (e.g., new customers only, specific products). Ensure the code is entered without extra spaces or typos. If all conditions are met, manually apply the discount to their account and proceed with the order.

Article 15: Title: Requesting Data Export or Deletion Content: Customers can request a full export of their data by contacting support, citing their intent. For account and data deletion, they must formally confirm this action, as it is irreversible. The request is then escalated to the Data Privacy team for handling within the legal timeframe.

Article 16: Title: Using the Search Function Effectively Content: The search bar supports partial matching and boolean operators (AND, OR, NOT). Advise users to enclose exact phrases in quotation marks (e.g., "annual report 2024") for better results. Filters are also available on the left sidebar to narrow down results by date or type.

Article 17: Title: How to Contact the Sales Team for an Upgrade Content: Customers interested in upgrading their plan or discussing enterprise options should be directed to the "Contact Sales" form on the pricing page. Collect their name, company, and primary interest, and tag the ticket as a Sales Lead for immediate hand-off.

Article 18: Title: Fixing "Invalid Credentials" Login Error Content: The "Invalid Credentials" message means the entered username/email and password do not match our records. Advise the user to carefully check for typos, correct capitalization, and confirm they are using the email registered with the service. If unsuccessful, proceed with a password reset.

Article 19: Title: Understanding the Annual vs. Monthly Billing Cycles Content: The annual plan offers a discounted rate compared to paying month-to-month and is billed once per year. The monthly plan offers greater flexibility but is billed every 30 days at the standard rate. Customers can switch between cycles in Subscription Details.

Article 20: Title: Troubleshooting Broken Image or Asset Links Content: If images or files aren't loading, the issue might be due to a failed upload or incorrect permissions. Ask the user to re-upload the asset if possible. If the asset was uploaded correctly, check the asset's access permissions; it might be set to private.

Article 21: Title: Setting Up and Managing User Roles/Permissions Content: Administrators can manage team roles (e.g., Admin, Editor, Viewer) in Team Settings > Users. Explain that only users with Admin rights can invite new members and modify existing permissions. Confirm the user understands the scope of each role.

Article 22: Title: Resolving "Subscription Expired" Message Content: This message appears when a recurring payment has failed and the grace period has ended. Direct the user to Account Settings > Billing to update their payment method immediately. Advise that service will resume automatically once a successful payment is processed.

Article 23: Title: Recovering a Deactivated Account Content: If an account was deactivated (by the user or for inactivity), it can often be reactivated within 90 days. Escalate the request to the Account Management team, who will verify the user's identity before initiating the reactivation process. Data recovery is not guaranteed after 90 days.

Article 24: Title: How to Report Abuse or Inappropriate Content Content: Users can report misconduct by using the "Report" feature linked next to the content or user profile. For severe issues, instruct them to email trust@service.com with links and screenshots for immediate review by the moderation team.

Article 25: Title: Troubleshooting Mobile App Freezing Content: When the app freezes, the user should first try waiting 30 seconds for it to recover. If it remains unresponsive, they must clear the app's cache in their device settings (iOS/Android) and restart their phone. Verify the device meets the minimum OS requirements.

Article 26: Title: Changing the Display Name and Profile Picture Content: The display name and profile picture can be customized in Account Settings > Profile. Images must be in JPG or PNG format and should not exceed 5MB. Remind the user that the display name is public-facing.

Article 27: Title: Addressing an Unexpected Charge on My Card Content: First, verify the charge amount and date against the customer's Billing History. Unrecognized charges are often renewal fees or an annual subscription they forgot about. If it is still unrecognized, confirm the last four digits of the card and check for related accounts.

Article 28: Title: How to Integrate with Third-Party Software (e.g., Zapier) Content: Integration setup is done via Settings > Integrations. The user must generate an API Key (see KB 13) and input it into the third-party application's connection settings. Provide a link to the specific integration guide in the developer documentation.

Article 29: Title: What to Do If My Account is Locked Content: An account is locked after five failed login attempts for security. The lock is usually temporary and lifts after 30 minutes. If the user needs immediate access, they must initiate a password reset, which also immediately unlocks the account.

Article 30: Title: Reporting a Bug or System Glitch Content: Encourage users to provide as much detail as possible when reporting bugs: device type, operating system, browser, steps to reproduce the error, and screenshots. This allows the engineering team to replicate and fix the issue quickly.

Article 33: Title: Adjusting the Time Zone Setting Content: The time zone for displaying activity logs and scheduled events is managed in Account Settings > Profile.
